# T10 — Born Rule (Gleason–Busch Reconstruction)

**Status:** FORCED-unconditional (ratified 2026-04-26).
**Layer:** Theorem-level structural result.

## Summary

States that the squared-amplitude probability rule `Prob(K) = b_K / Σ b` is FORCED as a structural consequence of the ED participation-graph ontology. Channels are ontologically primitive, bandwidth is intrinsic, and partition-independence (non-contextuality) is therefore not postulated but inherited from below. Combined with Gleason's theorem (and Busch's 2003 POVM extension covering qubits), the Born rule's squared form drops out as a derived rather than postulated rule.
