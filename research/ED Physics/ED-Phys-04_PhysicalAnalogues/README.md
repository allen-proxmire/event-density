# ED-Phys-04: Physical Analogues

## Purpose

Map ED simulation outputs to physical observables and standard cosmological quantities (Hubble parameter, scale factor, energy density, pressure, equation of state).

## Relationship to the ED-Phys Pipeline

- **Depends on:** ED-Phys-03 (Cosmological Timeline) for simulation histories; ED-Phys-01 (Update Rule) for the underlying dynamics.
- **Feeds into:** ED-Phys-07 (Analytical Theory) for validation against known cosmological laws; ED-Phys-08 (Documentation) for results reporting.

## Scope

- ED-to-Friedmann mapping (H, a, rho, p, w)
- Temporal tension analogues
- Horizon evolution tracking
- Comparison with standard Lambda-CDM predictions
