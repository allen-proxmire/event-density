1. PURPOSE OF ARCHITECTURAL SYNTHESIS
========================================================================

The first ten chapters of the ED-Arch series decomposed the unified
ED cosmology PDE into nine structural layers: motifs, laws, geometry,
field theory, energetics, causality, information, symmetry, and
invariants. Each chapter extracted one layer and analyzed it in
depth. This eleventh chapter reassembles the layers into a single
coherent system.

Architectural synthesis is:

  (i)   INTEGRATION.  Not a summary of the previous chapters, but
        a demonstration that they form a unified whole. Each layer
        is not an independent description but a facet of a single
        object: the ED architecture. Synthesis shows how the facets
        fit together.

  (ii)  COHERENCE.  The nine layers are not merely compatible; they
        are mutually constraining. The motifs imply the laws. The
        laws shape the geometry. The geometry hosts the field. The
        field drives the energetics. The energetics direct the
        causality. The causality processes the information. The
        information reveals the symmetry. The symmetry generates the
        invariants. And the invariants constrain the motifs. The
        layers form a closed, self-consistent system.

  (iii) IDENTITY.  The synthesis defines what the ED cosmology PDE
        IS, as an architectural entity. Not what it computes (that
        is the PDE's job), not what it produces (that is the
        simulation's job), but what it is structurally: a specific
        configuration of motifs, laws, geometry, fields, energetics,
        causality, information, symmetry, and invariants that
        together constitute a unique architectural identity.

Synthesis is the natural next step after invariants because the
invariants identified the architecture's irreducible core. The
synthesis shows how that core generates the complete architectural
structure through the interplay of all nine layers.

2. THE ARCHITECTURAL STACK
========================================================================

The ED architecture consists of nine layers, each building on and
constraining the others. The layers form a stack: each layer provides
the foundation for the next and is constrained by the layer above.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
THE NINE LAYERS
------------------------------------------------------------------------

Layer 1: MOTIFS (ED-Arch-02)
  The recurring structural patterns: Saddle, Spiral, Horizon,
  Manifold, Boundary, Memory Loop, Dissipator, Nonlinear Triad.
  Eight motifs that constitute the architecture's vocabulary.

Layer 2: LAWS (ED-Arch-03)
  The invariant constraints: Unique Attraction, One-Dimensional
  Relaxation, Proportional Stability, Critical Damping Boundary,
  Finite Capacity, Harmonic Protection, Modal Hierarchy, Channel
  Complementarity, Transient Structure. Nine laws organized as
  three axioms and six theorems.

Layer 3: GEOMETRY (ED-Arch-04)
  The shapes in state space: Attractor Point, Stable Manifold Curve,
  Spiral Sheet, Monotonic Cone, Horizon Surface, Boundary Surface,
  Modal Decay Funnel, Nonlinear Triad Locus. Eight geometric objects
  in a nested hierarchy.

Layer 4: FIELD THEORY (ED-Arch-05)
  The continuous field structure: density field (substance),
  participation field (memory), operator field (rule). Three coupled
  fields forming a single architectural field with four local
  encodings (curvature, mobility, restoring force, memory).

Layer 5: ENERGETICS (ED-Arch-06)
  The driving forces: Penalty Potential, Mobility Curvature Energy,
  Participation Memory Energy, Nonlinear Interaction Energy, Total
  Architectural Energy. Five functionals drained by three dissipation
  channels (D-channel, zeta-channel, P-channel).

Layer 6: CAUSALITY (ED-Arch-07)
  The influence pathways: spatial propagation (via mobility), temporal
  propagation (via participation), spectral propagation (via triad).
  Three channels governed by five propagation rules (Locality, Memory,
  Dissipation, Spectral, Capacity).

Layer 7: INFORMATION (ED-Arch-08)
  The structural content: spatial information (mode amplitudes),
  temporal information (participation memory), spectral information
  (harmonic relationships). Three forms processed through a nine-
  stage pipeline from routing to erasure.

Layer 8: SYMMETRY (ED-Arch-09)
  The invariances and breakings: five preserved symmetries (spatial
  translation, multiplicative channel, spectral phase, temporal
  reparameterization, amplitude scaling), five broken symmetries
  (time-reversal, density translation, amplitude scaling at capacity,
  oscillatory persistence, spectral permutation).

Layer 9: INVARIANTS (ED-Arch-10)
  The irreducible core: nine invariant quantities (unique attractor,
  1D manifold, linear boundary, locked harmonic ratio, bounded
  capacity, channel sum, modal ordering, clockwise winding, monotone
  direction), seven invariant relations, six equivalence classes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
INTER-LAYER DEPENDENCIES
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The layers are not independent. Each depends on and constrains the
others through specific structural relationships:

  MOTIFS depend on LAWS: each motif exists because specific laws
  permit it. The Spiral exists because the Critical Damping Boundary
  law permits oscillation below D + 2*zeta = 1. The Horizon exists
  because the Finite Capacity law creates a mobility zero.

  LAWS depend on GEOMETRY: each law implies a geometric object. The
  Law of Unique Attraction implies the Attractor Point. The Law of
  Critical Damping Boundary implies the Boundary Surface.

  GEOMETRY depends on FIELD THEORY: each geometric object is a
  feature of the architectural field. The Spiral Sheet is the set
  of spiral trajectories in the field. The Modal Funnel is the
  spectral structure of the field.

  FIELD THEORY depends on ENERGETICS: the field evolves by
  dissipating architectural energy. The field's update rule (F[rho])
  is the variational derivative of the energy functional.

  ENERGETICS depends on CAUSALITY: energy flows through causal
  channels. The three dissipation channels correspond to the three
  causal propagation mechanisms.

  CAUSALITY depends on INFORMATION: causal influence carries
  structural information. The causal channels are the information
  channels.

  INFORMATION depends on SYMMETRY: the information processing
  pipeline is constrained by symmetry. Multiplicative invariance
  means the pipeline's topology is fixed. Spectral phase symmetry
  means phases are informationally irrelevant.

  SYMMETRY depends on INVARIANTS: the symmetries are defined by
  what they leave invariant. The invariants are the fixed points
  of the symmetry group.

  INVARIANTS depend on MOTIFS: the invariants characterize the
  motifs. The manifold dimension (1) is the Manifold motif's
  invariant. The winding direction (clockwise) is the Spiral
  motif's invariant.

The dependencies form a cycle: Motifs -> Laws -> Geometry -> Field ->
Energetics -> Causality -> Information -> Symmetry -> Invariants ->
Motifs. This circularity is not a logical defect; it is the signature
of a self-consistent system. Each layer is both constrained by and
constraining of the others. The architecture is a closed, self-
supporting structure.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
DIRECTIONAL FLOW
------------------------------------------------------------------------

While the layers form a cycle in their structural dependencies, there
is a natural directional flow in terms of abstraction:

  CONCRETE ---------------------------------------------------> ABSTRACT
  Motifs -> Laws -> Geometry -> Field -> Energetics -> Causality
       -> Information -> Symmetry -> Invariants

Moving from left to right:
  - Specificity decreases (motifs are specific patterns; invariants
    are universal properties).
  - Generality increases (motifs apply to specific regimes; invariants
    apply to the entire architecture).
  - Transformability increases (motifs have specific shapes; invariants
    are unchanged under all transformations).

The synthesis integrates this spectrum from concrete to abstract,
showing that both ends describe the same architecture at different
levels of resolution.

4. ARCHITECTURAL FLOW
========================================================================

The ED architecture processes every initial condition through a
definite sequence of stages. Each stage involves specific layers of
the architectural stack. The flow is the architecture in action: the
dynamic process through which all layers cooperate to transform an
arbitrary initial state into the universal equilibrium.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 1: MODAL FUNNEL COLLAPSE
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: High-k spatial modes decay rapidly (rate proportional
  to k^2), leaving only the k=1 fundamental and its enslaved
  harmonics.

Layers involved:
  - Law: Modal Hierarchy (k^2 ordering drives selective decay)
  - Geometry: Modal Funnel (nested ellipsoids narrow)
  - Field: Linearized eigenfields decay independently
  - Energetics: V_M decreases as gradients smooth; D-channel primary
  - Causality: Spectral causal narrowing; high-k influence lost
  - Information: Hierarchical compression; spatial detail erased
  - Symmetry: Spectral permutation symmetry broken by k^2 ordering
  - Invariant: Modal decay ordering preserved throughout

Duration: approximately 1/(3*lambda_1) time units for 95% compression
  (3 e-folding times of the k=2/k=1 ratio).

Quantitative: ED-Phys-29 shows 22 IC types converging to the same
  manifold with convergence rate std/mean = 5.7-25% depending on
  cosmology. The residual variation is the fingerprint of Stage 1.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 2: MANIFOLD CAPTURE
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: The trajectory reaches the 1D manifold curve. The
  effective dynamical dimension drops from N/2 to 1 (or 2, counting
  the associated v projection).

Layers involved:
  - Motif: Manifold (the trajectory locks onto the 1D curve)
  - Law: One-Dimensional Relaxation (spectral gap ensures capture)
  - Geometry: Stable Manifold Curve (the trajectory joins it)
  - Field: Dominant eigenfield pair captures the dynamics
  - Information: Compression from N/2 to 1 dimension complete

Duration: coincident with Stage 1 (the Modal Funnel's output IS the
  Manifold).

Quantitative: manifold dimension = 1 in 64/66 runs (ED-Phys-29),
  48/48 runs (ED-Phys-30).

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 3: SPIRAL OR MONOTONIC APPROACH
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: The trajectory moves along the Manifold Curve toward
  the Attractor Point. The approach is either spiral (below the
  Boundary) or monotonic (above it).

Layers involved:
  - Motif: Spiral or Dissipator (depending on regime)
  - Motif: Memory Loop (active in spiral regime, H > 0)
  - Law: Critical Damping Boundary (determines regime)
  - Geometry: Spiral Sheet or Monotonic Cone
  - Field: Complex eigenfield pair (spiral) or real eigenfield
    (monotonic)
  - Energetics: Oscillatory circulation (spiral) or direct drainage
    (monotonic)
  - Causality: Helical propagation (spiral) or radial flow (monotonic)
  - Information: Phase-encoded delay (spiral) or direct erasure
    (monotonic)
  - Symmetry: Rotational symmetry (spiral) or conical scaling
    (monotonic)
  - Invariant: Winding direction (spiral); monotonicity (monotonic)

Duration: 1 to 235 cycles (spiral) or continuous monotonic decay
  (monotonic). Total time: 272 to 1360 time units depending on
  regime and amplitude.

Quantitative: cycle counts from ED-Phys-31 (mean 0 to 49 depending
  on D and zeta). Convergence rates from ED-Phys-33 (lambda = 0.006
  to 0.139).

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 4: HORIZON ENCOUNTERS (CONDITIONAL)
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: If D = 0 and initial amplitude A >= 40, the trajectory
  reaches the Horizon Surface. Diffusion stalls. The penalty and
  participation channels continue operating. The trajectory is
  reflected back.

Layers involved:
  - Motif: Horizon (mobility collapse activates)
  - Law: Finite Capacity (M -> 0)
  - Geometry: Horizon Surface (trajectory touches it)
  - Field: Mobility field zero set encountered
  - Energetics: D-channel blocked; V_M stalls; P-channel and
    zeta-channel continue
  - Causality: Partial barrier; spatial propagation blocked
  - Information: Truncation; density capped at capacity
  - Symmetry: Saturation symmetry active
  - Invariant: Capacity bound rho_max never exceeded

Duration: brief (fraction of one oscillation period). Residence
  time at the Horizon is short.

Quantitative: horizon activated in 8/22 oscillatory runs in
  ED-Phys-29 (36%), always at A >= 40. Horizon_max = 1 to 7 density
  units above threshold.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 5: ENERGY DISSIPATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: The three dissipation channels drain all forms of
  architectural energy. V_P, V_M, V_v all approach zero.

Layers involved:
  - Motif: Dissipator (terminal processing)
  - Law: Transient Structure (all energy dissipates)
  - Geometry: Trajectory approaches Attractor Point
  - Field: All field components approach equilibrium values
  - Energetics: E[rho, v] -> 0 monotonically
  - Causality: All causal influence absorbed by the sink
  - Information: All information erased
  - Symmetry: Broken symmetries in the process of restoration
  - Invariant: Energy monotonicity (dE/dt <= 0) maintained throughout

Duration: extends from the beginning of the dynamics to the end.
  Energy dissipation is not a single stage but a continuous process
  active during all other stages. It becomes the dominant process
  at late times when the specific motif-level dynamics (spirals,
  horizons) have subsided.

Quantitative: final_std ~ 10^-14 across all experiments, confirming
  essentially complete dissipation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 6: INFORMATION COMPRESSION
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: The nine-stage information pipeline processes the
  initial condition from high complexity to zero complexity.

Layers involved:
  - Information: routing (Saddle), mixing (Triad), compression (Modal
    Funnel), storage (Memory Loop), feedback (H*v), delay (Spiral),
    truncation (Horizon), collapse (Manifold), erasure (Dissipator)
  - All other layers participate through their informational roles

Duration: concurrent with all other stages. Information compression
  begins at t=0 (high-k modes start decaying immediately) and ends
  at equilibrium (zero information remaining).

Quantitative: effective information dimension drops from approximately
  64 (for N_x = 128) to 1 (on the manifold) to 0 (at equilibrium).

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 7: SYMMETRY RESTORATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: The field moves from a low-symmetry initial state
  (non-uniform density, nonzero velocity, active gradients) toward
  the maximally symmetric equilibrium (uniform density, zero velocity,
  no gradients).

Layers involved:
  - Symmetry: broken symmetries are restored as structure is erased
    - Density translation restored (rho -> rho* everywhere)
    - Amplitude scaling restored (amplitude -> 0, below any threshold)
    - Oscillatory persistence restored trivially (v -> 0)
    - Spectral permutation restored trivially (all modes -> 0)
  - Time-reversal symmetry remains broken throughout (the process is
    irreversible even as it restores other symmetries)

Duration: concurrent with energy dissipation. Symmetry is restored
  progressively, not all at once.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
STAGE 8: INVARIANT CONVERGENCE
------------------------------------------------------------------------

What happens: The trajectory converges to the state that manifests
  all invariants simultaneously. At equilibrium:
  - The attractor location is realized (rho = rho*, v = 0).
  - The manifold dimension is trivially satisfied (a point is 0D,
    which is consistent with lying on a 1D curve).
  - The capacity bound is satisfied (rho* < rho_max).
  - The channel sum D + H = 1 continues to hold.
  - The modal ordering is trivially satisfied (all amplitudes zero).
  - The energy monotonicity reaches its final state (E = 0, dE/dt = 0).

Layers involved:
  - Invariants: all invariants satisfied simultaneously
  - All other layers: the invariants are properties of all layers

Duration: the terminal state. Reached asymptotically.

5. THE ARCHITECTURAL CANON
========================================================================

The full nine-layer description is rich but redundant: many features
at higher layers are derivable from features at lower layers. The
architectural canon is the minimal set of structural principles from
which the entire architecture can be derived. It is the architecture's
axiom set: the smallest collection of premises that generates all
motifs, laws, geometry, fields, energetics, causality, information,
symmetry, and invariants.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
THE SEVEN CANONICAL PRINCIPLES
------------------------------------------------------------------------

PRINCIPLE 1: THE OPERATOR STRUCTURE
  F[rho] = M(rho)*nabla^2(rho) + M'(rho)|nabla(rho)|^2 - P_SY2(rho)

  The operator is a nonlinear diffusion with penalty. This three-term
  structure generates: the Saddle (from the curvature and gradient
  terms), the Nonlinear Triad (from the M' coupling), the Dissipator
  (from the diffusive character), and the penalty equilibrium (from
  P_SY2's zero at rho*).

PRINCIPLE 2: CHANNEL COMPLEMENTARITY
  drho/dt = D*F[rho] + H*v, with D + H = 1.

  The density evolution is split between a direct channel (D*F) and
  a mediated channel (H*v). This split generates: the three
  cosmologies (D=0, 0.5, 1), the tradeoff between oscillation and
  dissipation, and the effective parameter-space simplex.

PRINCIPLE 3: THE PENALTY EQUILIBRIUM
  P_SY2(rho*) = 0, with P_SY2 monotonically increasing in rho.

  The penalty has a unique zero. This generates: the Law of Unique
  Attraction, the Attractor Point, the direction of becoming, and
  the global convergence.

PRINCIPLE 4: THE MOBILITY CAPACITY BOUND
  M(rho_max) = 0, with M(rho) > 0 for rho < rho_max.

  The mobility vanishes at the density ceiling. This generates: the
  Horizon motif, the Horizon Surface, the Finite Capacity law, the
  bounded state space, and the saturation symmetry.

PRINCIPLE 5: THE MEMORY LOOP TOPOLOGY
  dv/dt = (1/tau)*(F[rho] - zeta*v).

  The participation field integrates F with exponential decay. This
  generates: the Memory Loop motif, temporal causality, participation
  energy, oscillatory dynamics, and the spiral approach.

PRINCIPLE 6: THE TRIAD COUPLING
  M'(rho)|nabla(rho)|^2 is a bilinear form on the gradient field.

  The nonlinear coupling has a specific algebraic structure. This
  generates: the Nonlinear Triad motif, the k=3 harmonic, the
  invariant triad ratio, the spectral causal channel, and the
  information mixing.

PRINCIPLE 7: THE DAMPING DISCRIMINANT
  The effective damping D + 2*zeta determines the flow type.

  The combined effect of direct dissipation (D) and participation
  damping (2*zeta) has a critical threshold at 1. This generates:
  the Boundary motif, the Boundary Surface, the Critical Damping
  Boundary law, the spiral-monotonic transition, the causal regime
  switch, and the information encoding switch.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY THESE SEVEN FORM THE CANONICAL CORE
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The seven canonical principles are irreducible: removing any one of
them would eliminate at least one motif, one law, one geometric
object, and one invariant.

  Remove Principle 1 (operator structure): lose the Saddle, the
    Triad, the Dissipator, and the entire spatial dynamics.
  Remove Principle 2 (channel complementarity): lose the three
    cosmologies, the D+H=1 constraint, and the parameter-space
    simplex.
  Remove Principle 3 (penalty equilibrium): lose the unique
    attractor, the direction of becoming, and global convergence.
  Remove Principle 4 (capacity bound): lose the Horizon, the bounded
    state space, and the saturation symmetry.
  Remove Principle 5 (memory loop): lose the Spiral, the participation
    field, temporal causality, and oscillatory dynamics.
  Remove Principle 6 (triad coupling): lose the k=3 harmonic, the
    spectral fingerprint, and the nonlinear mode mixing.
  Remove Principle 7 (damping discriminant): lose the Boundary, the
    spiral-monotonic transition, and the causal regime switch.

The seven principles are also sufficient: from them, the entire
nine-layer architecture can be derived. The laws follow from the
principles' mathematical structure. The geometry follows from the
laws' implications for state space. The field theory IS the
principles expressed as a field. The energetics follow from the
field's variational structure. The causality follows from the field's
coupling topology. The information follows from the causal channels'
processing capacity. The symmetry follows from the principles'
transformation properties. The invariants follow from the symmetry's
fixed points.

The seven principles are the architecture's genome: the minimal
specification from which the complete organism develops.

7. ARCHITECTURAL IDENTITY
========================================================================

The synthesis of all nine layers defines the architecture's identity:
what makes the ED cosmology PDE the specific architectural system
that it is.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.1 WHAT MUST BE PRESERVED
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The architecture's identity is maintained as long as the following
properties hold:

  A unique point attractor at (rho*, 0).
  A one-dimensional relaxation manifold.
  A linear critical damping boundary at D + 2*zeta = 1.
  A locked harmonic ratio k3/k1.
  A bounded state space with capacity limit rho_max.
  Channel complementarity D + H = 1.
  An ordered modal hierarchy with k^2 spectral decay.
  A clockwise spiral winding (when spirals exist).
  A monotone direction of becoming (dE/dt <= 0).
  Three coupled fields: density, participation, operator.
  Three dissipation channels: D, zeta, P.
  Eight motifs: Saddle, Spiral, Horizon, Manifold, Boundary,
    Memory Loop, Dissipator, Nonlinear Triad.
  Nine laws: Unique Attraction, One-Dimensional Relaxation,
    Proportional Stability, Critical Damping Boundary, Finite
    Capacity, Harmonic Protection, Modal Hierarchy, Channel
    Complementarity, Transient Structure.

Any PDE instance that preserves all of these properties is
architecturally identical to the canonical ED cosmology PDE,
regardless of the specific functional forms of M, P, or the
v-coupling.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.2 WHAT CAN VARY WITHOUT CHANGING IDENTITY
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The following properties can vary freely without altering the
architecture's identity:

  The specific functional form of M(rho), provided it satisfies
    M(rho_max) = 0 and M(rho) > 0 for rho < rho_max.
  The specific functional form of P_SY2(rho), provided it has a
    unique zero at rho* and is monotonically increasing.
  The specific values of D, zeta, tau (within their physical ranges).
  The specific initial condition.
  The specific amplitude of initial deviations (within the capacity
    bound).
  The specific spatial domain length L.
  The specific grid resolution N_x.
  The numerical values of alpha, gamma, M0, rho_max, rho*, rho_0
    (provided the qualitative structure is preserved).

These are the architecture's parameters: the details that can change
without changing the architecture. They are the coordinates on the
multiplicative equivalence class.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
7.3 WHAT WOULD BREAK THE ARCHITECTURE
------------------------------------------------------------------------

The architecture would be broken (cease to be ED) by any modification
that violates one or more invariants:

  ADDING A SECOND EQUILIBRIUM: a penalty function with two zeros
    would create a second attractor, violating Unique Attraction.
    The architecture would become bistable. The manifold could
    bifurcate. The direction of becoming would no longer be unique.

  REMOVING THE CAPACITY BOUND: a mobility that is positive everywhere
    (M(rho) > 0 for all rho) would eliminate the Horizon, allow
    unbounded density, and remove the Finite Capacity law. The
    architecture would lose its saturation mechanism.

  BREAKING THE SPECTRAL GAP: a differential operator with eigenvalues
    that do not scale as k^2 (e.g., a first-order advection operator)
    would eliminate the Modal Hierarchy, prevent manifold collapse to
    1D, and break the information compression pipeline. The
    architecture would lose its dimensional reduction.

  ADDING AN ENERGY SOURCE: a forcing term that injects energy into
    the density or participation field from outside the architecture
    would break Transient Structure by allowing sustained or growing
    oscillations. This is precisely what additive perturbations do
    (ED-Phys-27, 28): they inject energy through a channel not
    controlled by the architecture's dissipation mechanisms.

  REMOVING THE PARTICIPATION COUPLING: setting H = 0 permanently
    (not as a parameter choice but as a structural constraint)
    would eliminate the Memory Loop, the Spiral, the participation
    field, and temporal causality. The architecture would reduce to
    a pure nonlinear diffusion with penalty — still convergent, still
    dissipative, but without the oscillatory repertoire that defines
    ED's richness.

  BREAKING THE LINEAR DAMPING: a v-equation with nonlinear damping
    (e.g., zeta*v^3 instead of zeta*v) would bend the Critical
    Damping Boundary from a plane into a surface, potentially
    destroying the Boundary's linearity invariant. The architecture's
    parameter-space geometry would change qualitatively.

The ED architecture is coherent because its nine layers are not
independent descriptions but mutually constraining aspects of a
single system. The coherence manifests in three ways:

  VERTICAL COHERENCE: each layer is derivable from the layers below
  it. Motifs generate laws. Laws shape geometry. Geometry hosts
  fields. Fields drive energetics. Energetics direct causality.
  Causality processes information. Information reveals symmetry.
  Symmetry generates invariants. No layer contradicts any other.

  HORIZONTAL COHERENCE: each motif has a consistent expression across
  all nine layers. The Spiral is simultaneously a motif (oscillatory
  convergence), a law consequence (sub-critical damping), a geometric

  no layer floats free, and no layer can be removed without
